    • Title
      Fixed Site/Field Camp/Ship
      2007-2037: Waste management plan for the J.G. Mendel Czech Antarctic Station
      Johann Gregor Mendel Czech Antarctic Station
      Objective
      Waste Management Plan (WMP) is designed for the J.G. Mendel Czech Antarctic Station and summarized in related CEE “Czech Scientific Station in Antarctica – Construction and Operation, Czech Republic, January 2004”. The WMP aims to provide guidelines on waste reduction, segregation, collection and disposal practices in accordance with international best practices, to avoid deterioration of the natural environment and negative impacts on the health and safety in the area.

      Türkiye
      Parties involved
      Czechia
      Subject matter
      Science, Logistics, General
      Description
      A Memorandum of Understanding was signed between the Ministry of Industry and Technology of the Republic of Türkiye and the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports of the Czechia, on July 8, 2019 in Prague. The context of cooperation includes the following areas: -Logistic support; -Joint studies in the field of natural sciences; -Protection of Antarctic environment; -Exchange of information on the domestic laws and regulations on Antarctica.
