      Scope/coverage of the plan
      Contingency Plan for oil spills at Concordia Station
      There is no plan publically available at the moment but procedures exist, largely following those recommended by COMNAP. The plan is regularly tested by theoretical training of staff and practical exercises on spill response. The attached document is a list of equipment available at Concordia to used in case of oil spill.
      Objective
      The plan identifies the nature and size of the possible threat and of the resources most at risk. It lists the response equipment available on site. It defines the functions and responsibilities of staff on site. It describes response actions. It list the cleaning and regular maintenance operation of the equipment.

      Description
