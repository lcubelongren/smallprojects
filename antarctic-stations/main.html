A map of all Antarctic places documented by the Secretariat of the Antarctic Treaty. Each participating country's Permanent Information document is referenced and every listed location mapped. This information is reported as a requirement of Article VII of the Treaty.

Click the flags and toggles or mouse over each dot to explore the continent.

Not all Antarctic locations are included, however many are.

Enjoy exploring! ❄
